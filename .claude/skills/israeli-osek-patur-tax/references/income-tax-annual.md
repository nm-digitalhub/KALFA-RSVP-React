# Income tax for the self-employed עוסק פטור — verified reference (2026)

Figures verified 2026-07-18 (see `../evidence.json`). Brackets/values change
annually (2026 had a MID-YEAR retroactive reform) — always re-verify.

## Annual return (דוח שנתי)

- Form 1301, online filing mandatory for the self-employed; includes business
  income, all other income, and spouse where relevant.
- Tax-year-2025 deadlines fell April–June 2026 (30.6.2026 for online filers per
  the published notice), with מייצגים extensions beyond. Deadlines are
  published EVERY YEAR — never assume last year's date.
- מקדמות: set at year start from the prior assessment/estimate, paid
  periodically, reconciled in the annual return; a new/small business can
  request a reduction.
- Withholding (ניכוי במקור): business customers must withhold unless the עוסק
  holds a valid exemption certificate (issued in the personal area, renewed
  annually). B2C receipts from individuals (KALFA's case) are not subject to
  withholding.

## 2026 brackets (earned income, post the 30.3.2026 widening reform / Tiqun 288)

Operative annual table (≥2 identical published sources):
10% ≤ 84,120 · 14% ≤ 120,720 · 20% ≤ 228,000 · 31% ≤ 301,200 ·
35% ≤ 560,280 · 47% above · +3% surtax (threshold reported 721,560; one
source claims 765,390 — conflict, verify before use).
**TRAP:** the live Nevo §121 text prints pre-indexation base amounts for the
low brackets (74,640/107,040) that are NOT operative, while the reform-widened
brackets (228,000/301,200/560,280) do match — never quote bracket thresholds
from the statute without cross-checking the Tax Authority table. Indexation
frozen until the 2027 index per the amendment.
Credit point 2026: **242 ₪/month (2,904/yr)**; resident 2.25 points =
6,534 ₪/yr; women +0.5. (The §33א statutory base of 504 ₪ is NOT the
operative value — same trap.)

## עסק זעיר — the tiny-business regime (פרק שמיני, §87ב–87ה)

- Eligibility: individual resident, turnover ≤ the עוסק-פטור VAT ceiling
  (same figure exactly, by reference).
- **An עוסק פטור is registered as בעל עסק זעיר AUTOMATICALLY by פקיד השומה**
  (§87ג(א)) and is notified with a right to opt out.
- Effect (§87ד): an automatic deduction of **30% of turnover** replaces actual
  expenses — §§17–27 deductions do NOT apply (except §17(5א), hishtalmut) and
  §47א (national-insurance deduction) does NOT apply.
- Decision each year: 30% of turnover vs. (actual expenses + 52% BTL deduction
  + other deductions). Low-expense SaaS usually benefits from the automatic
  30%, but compute with real numbers — CPA-question when close.

## Recognized expenses (only when NOT under עסק זעיר)

From the expense-deduction regulations (תקנות ניכוי הוצאות מסוימות, fetched
live — `law_html/law01/255_418.htm`):
- Home landline: deductible only if home proven as the primary place of
  business; ≤18,000 ₪/yr → deduct 80% OR (cost − 1,800 ₪), the LOWER;
  >18,000 ₪/yr → deduct (cost − 3,600 ₪).
- Vehicle: capped at 2/3 of maintenance amounts (licensing, insurance,
  depreciation, fuel, repairs).
- Mobile phone: capped at 960 ₪ or half the expense, the LOWER.
- Computer depreciation: 33%/yr straight-line (SEARCH-CURRENT — depreciation
  regulations not fetched live).
- Proportional home-office utilities (arnona/electricity/internet by m²):
  professional PRACTICE, not codified — present as convention, not law.
- Cloud/servers/SaaS subscriptions: ordinary fully-deductible business expense
  under the §17 general test; no special sub-regulation.
Foreign-supplier costs (Voximplant, Meta) are a declared CPA-question for
classification.

## Bookkeeping (הוראות ניהול פנקסים)

- A service business with no dedicated schedule falls under **תוספת י"א —
  "נותני שירותים ואחרים"**. Tiers: >2,150,000 ₪ → double-entry;
  820,000–2,150,000 ₪ (or ≥5 employees) → mid-tier; **≤820,000 ₪ AND <5
  employees → minimal: receipts-and-payments ledger + receipt vouchers +
  order book + invoice for any service ≥210 ₪** — an עוסק פטור is always in
  this tier. §2א adds tiny-business relief. Computerized-ledger legitimacy is
  governed by a separate instrument (not fetched — open item).
- KALFA: SUMIT is the computerized document/ledger system; one-time CPA
  confirmation that it satisfies every תוספת-י"א requirement is declared in
  the shared tax catalog.
