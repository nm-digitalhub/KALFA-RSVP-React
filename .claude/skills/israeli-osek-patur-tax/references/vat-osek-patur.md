# VAT / עוסק פטור — verified reference (2026)

All figures verified 2026-07-18; see `../evidence.json` for exact quotes + sources.
Everything here is indexed/amended annually — re-verify in a new tax year.

## Status and ceiling

- Definition + ceiling live INSIDE the statute: חוק מע"מ תשל"ו-1975 §1 — "עוסק
  פטור – עוסק שמחזור העסקאות שלו בכל עסקיו אינו עולה על **122,833 ₪** לשנה"
  (2026; 2025: 120,000 ₪). Turnover (מחזור), not profit, across ALL businesses.
- Exemption base: §31(3) — transactions of an עוסק פטור are exempt, EXCEPT real
  estate and equipment whose input VAT was deducted.
- Consequences: no VAT charged to customers, no שורת מע"מ on any document, no
  input-VAT (מס תשומות) deduction on purchases.
- Mid-year business opening: ceiling is prorated (תקנה 14 לתקנות הרישום —
  month×12 / estimate).
- Excluded occupations (תקנה 13 לתקנות הרישום): liberal professions (אדריכל,
  עו"ד, רו"ח, יועץ ניהול…) + תקנה 6א services (lectures, writing, editing,
  translation, performances, exams) must be עוסק מורשה. Software/SaaS is on
  NEITHER list — a SaaS operator may be עוסק פטור.

## Annual obligations

- **הצהרת עוסק פטור: by 31 January** each year, on the previous calendar year's
  turnover (תקנה 15 לתקנות הרישום), filed in the רשות המסים personal area.
- No periodic VAT returns, no VAT bookkeeping beyond the receipts trail.

## Documents

- **קבלה — mandatory immediately on EVERY payment received** (bookkeeping rules).
- **חשבונית עסקה — statutorily MANDATORY for every transaction, even exempt
  ones** (§45, live quote: "עוסק חייב להוציא לקונה חשבונית עסקה על כל עסקה או
  חלק מעסקה גם אם הם פטורים ממס"). Common practice for an עוסק פטור is
  receipt-only — the §45-vs-practice gap is a declared CPA-question; a combined
  "חשבונית עסקה/קבלה" document satisfies both.
- **חשבונית מס — PROHIBITED**: §47(א) reserves it to עוסק מורשה; unauthorized
  issuance is a criminal offense, up to 1 year imprisonment (§117(א)(5)).
- חשבוניות ישראל / allocation numbers: applies to tax invoices only (1.1.2026:
  >10,000 ₪; 1.6.2026: >5,000 ₪ pre-VAT) — **not applicable** to an עוסק פטור.

## Crossing the ceiling → עוסק מורשה

1. Monitor turnover continuously (KALFA: SUMIT charge records are the source).
2. Before receiving the payment that crosses the ceiling, register the status
   change at the regional VAT office (client's office: אשדוד): form 821 + ID
   copy + business documents (office list: Registration Regulations addendum,
   per regulation 16).
3. From the change onward: charge 18% VAT, issue tax invoices, periodic returns.
4. Input VAT from the exempt period is NOT retroactively deductible; the
   prospective-only VAT treatment of the excess amount itself is practitioner
   consensus — no explicit statutory clause located (CPA-question).
5. **Myth (do not repeat): "up to 25% overshoot lets you stay exempt until year
   end" — no verified source supports this.** If it comes up, treat as a
   CPA-question, not fact.
6. Cascades on status change: accessibility exemption (built on the עוסק-פטור
   certificate) must be re-evaluated; B2C pricing must absorb/display VAT;
   the SUMIT document template changes from receipt-only.
